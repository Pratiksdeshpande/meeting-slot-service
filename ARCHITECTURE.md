## ğŸ¯ Technology Decisions

| Component | Options Considered | Choice | Rationale |
|-----------|-------------------|--------|-----------|
| Web Framework | net/http, Gin, Mux, Echo | **Gorilla Mux** | Standard, lightweight, excellent routing, no bloat |

| Container | Docker, Podman | **Docker** | Industry standard, best tooling |

| IaC | Terraform, CloudFormation, Pulumi | **Terraform** | Multi-cloud capable, declarative, mature |
